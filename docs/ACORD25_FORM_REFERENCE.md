## State-Specific Requirements

### New York (NY)
- All employees must be covered under NYS Workers Compensation Law
- NYS Disability Benefits Insurance required
- DBL Policy # and Carrier information required

### New Jersey (NJ)
- Coverage under NJ Workers Compensation Law
- NJ Temporary Disability Benefits Law coverage required

### Connecticut (CT)
- CT Workers Compensation Act compliance required

### Pennsylvania (PA)
- PA Workers Compensation Act compliance required
